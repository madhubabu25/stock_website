Great Investors

Warren Buffett

Warren Buffett is considered one of the most successful investors of all time. He is known for his value investing philosophy and for being the chairman and CEO of Berkshire Hathaway.

Charlie Munger

Charlie Munger is an investor and vice chairman of Berkshire Hathaway. He is known for his brilliant mind and sharp insights into the world of investing.

Ray Dalio

Ray Dalio is the founder of Bridgewater Associates, one of the world's largest hedge funds. He has a strong track record of making long-term successful investments.
